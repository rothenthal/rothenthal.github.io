Event Overview

From ESG
Requirements to
Building Reality

This session is not about explaining ESG and regulatory basics. It is about understanding what clients, investors and regulators are truly asking for — and where the CREE System already delivers strong answers. And just as important: where do we need strategic partners, where are the current gaps, and how do we position ourselves as a network?

This event will feature a presentation followed by an engaging discussion, allowing participants to share experiences, insights, and best practices.

Agenda Highlights

What We'll
Cover

01

EU Regulatory Framework

An overview of the current EU regulatory landscape for ESG in the building sector, and what it means in practice for CREE partners.

02

Member State Snapshots

Implementation snapshots across member states — how different markets are translating EU-level directives into local requirements.

03

Relevance to Buildings

How ESG regulation directly impacts building design, construction and operations — and why timber hybrid systems have an inherent advantage.

04

DGNB as a Bridge

DGNB certification as the connecting layer between regulatory frameworks and real building performance — and how CREE aligns.

05

Where CREE Fits

The strengths of the CREE System in an ESG context — embodied carbon, material efficiency, speed, and circularity potential.

06

The Honest Gap

Operational phase data, knowledge flow, and the need for strategic allies — an honest look at where the network must grow together.
